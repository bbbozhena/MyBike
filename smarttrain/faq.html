Frequently Asked Questions

What bikes are available on MyBike?

MyBike offers three bike models: Sporty 4, Ride in town ST, and Agile ride 3.

Which bike is best for city comfort?

Ride in town ST is best for comfort because it has an open frame and upright riding position.

Which bike is lightweight?

Agile ride 3 is a lightweight bike designed for agile and fast city riding.

Does MyBike support bike comparison?

Yes, users can compare different bikes and their characteristics on the website.

What smart features do MyBike bikes have?

MyBike bikes include auto unlock via mobile app, GPS tracking, integrated lights, and hydraulic disc brakes.

What is the battery range?

The removable battery provides up to 70 km of range and weighs 2.4 kg.
