Frequently Asked Questions about MyBike:

What bikes are available on MyBike?
MyBike offers three bike models: Sporty 4, Ride in town ST, and Agile ride 3.

Which bike is best for comfortable city riding?
Ride in town ST is best for comfort because it has an open frame and upright riding position.

Which bike is lightweight?
Agile ride 3 is the lightweight bike designed for agile city riding.

Can I compare bikes on the website?
Yes, MyBike allows users to compare different bike models and their features.

What smart features do the bikes have?
The bikes support auto unlock via a mobile app, GPS tracking, integrated lights,
and hydraulic disc brakes.

What is the battery range?
The removable battery provides up to 70 km of range and weighs 2.4 kg.

Do the bikes have integrated lights?
Yes, all bikes have lights integrated into the frame for day and night visibility.
